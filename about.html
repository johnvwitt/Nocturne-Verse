Why We Exist

Nocturne Verse was born from the quiet hours — a digital refuge for poetry that haunts, heals, and whispers back. We believe poetry is not a luxury but a survival skill, and our mission is to elevate voices that explore the dark, the dreamy, and the divine. Whether your work emerges from grief, myth, silence, or joy — if it glows in the night, we want to read it.

What We Publish

Our focus is contemporary poetry that leans lyrical, speculative, gothic, or experimental. We look for:

  • Poems that shimmer with emotional resonance
  • Voices from the margins — of culture, genre, and genre-defying form
  • Visceral imagery, mythic tone, or unexpected vulnerability
  • Short poetic forms, cycles, or prose poems welcome

We publish new work monthly. Two featured authors are selected for each issue, with occasional guest spotlights and editor’s notes.

Submission Guidelines

Submissions are currently open. Please send:

  • 3–5 original poems in a single document (.doc, .pdf, or .txt)
  • A brief third-person bio (100 words or fewer)
  • Optional: a short note on your creative intent or influences

We do not accept previously published work at this time. Simultaneous submissions are fine — just notify us if accepted elsewhere.

Submit via Submittable. Response time is typically 2–4 weeks.
