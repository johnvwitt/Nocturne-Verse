Submission Guidelines

We accept original poetry that leans lyrical, speculative, gothic, or experimental. Please read our guidelines before submitting:

  • Submit 3–5 original poems in one document (.doc, .docx, .pdf, or .txt)
  • Include a brief third-person bio (100 words or fewer)
  • Optional: include a note on your creative intent or process
  • Simultaneous submissions are okay — notify us if accepted elsewhere
  • No previously published work at this time

Response time is 2–4 weeks. We look forward to reading your work.
