About the Author

"Poetry is the fog of the soul lifting just long enough to see the shape of your longing."

Elias Thorn is a contemporary elegist with roots in the mist-shrouded foothills of Appalachia. His work reflects the quiet tension between memory and decay, often exploring nature as a vessel for sorrow and spiritual reclamation. With a background in folklore and environmental studies, Elias weaves lush visual textures with crisp emotional cuts. His poetry has appeared in Ember & Ash, The Vale Review, and now, Nocturne Verse.
