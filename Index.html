In the Veil of Night

Whispers thread the trees’ embrace,
Beneath the stars, a ghostly trace.
The moon weeps silver on the stone,
Where dreams and dust remain alone.

by A. Everhart

The Raven’s Lament

Crimson echoes, cold winds cry,
Through chapel ruins, shadows fly.
A velvet hush, a fleeting glance,
The night begins its mournful dance.

by L. Virelle
